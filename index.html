A quick look at who I am, what drives me, and where I'm headed.

I'm Chijioke Daniel, an Electrical & Electronics Engineering student at the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB), Nigeria.

Alongside my degree, I work as a frontend developer, building clean, responsive, and purposeful web interfaces. I specialize in turning complex ideas into seamless digital experiences, taking pride in writing efficient code that bridges the gap between high-end design and functional performance.

Right now, my passion is to learn how to combine electronics and software together—specifically through embedded systems and IoT—to solve real-world problems. I love the challenge of making hardware and code work as one, and I'm dedicated to growing my skills in this space to build technology that actually makes a difference.
