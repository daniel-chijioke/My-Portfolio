About Me

Engineer. Developer.
Product Thinker.

A quick look at who I am, what drives me, and where I'm headed.

I'm Chijioke Daniel, a 300-level Electrical & Electronics Engineering student at the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB), Nigeria.

Alongside my degree, I work as a self-employed frontend developer and data entry specialist — crafting web interfaces that are purposeful, accessible, and visually sharp. I take pride in writing clean code and delivering real results.

My long-term vision sits at the intersection of electronics and software — designing IoT products and consumer electronics that solve real problems. I believe the most impactful engineers think across the full stack: from circuit to cloud.
